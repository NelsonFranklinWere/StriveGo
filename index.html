About Us

The Hidden Gem Behind StriveGo

We Help Kenyan Businesses Grow Faster with Technology

StriveGo was founded by four ambitious Kenyan IT professionals who saw that many businesses in Kenya struggle with high operational costs, limited customer reach, and the inability to compete with larger companies. We decided to solve this by creating affordable AI and automation solutions that help small businesses grow faster.

Our mission is to empower Kenyan SMEs and startups by automating their manual tasks, reducing costs, and helping them increase sales through technology. We believe every business, regardless of size, should have access to the same tools that help big companies succeed - AI automation, professional websites, mobile apps, and digital marketing solutions.

We're not just building software; we're helping Kenyan entrepreneurs grow their businesses, create jobs, and compete in the digital economy. We understand the challenges businesses face in Kenya, and we build solutions specifically designed for the Kenyan market, including M-Pesa integration and local payment methods.

Our Vision: Every Kenyan Business Should Grow with Technology

We envision a future where every Kenyan business, no matter how small, can compete effectively using AI and automation. We want to see small businesses increase their sales, reduce costs, and serve customers 24/7 - just like big companies do, but at an affordable price.

We focus on building practical, affordable, and scalable tech products - WhatsApp bots for automated customer service, websites that make sales, mobile apps that improve customer experience, and business automation tools that reduce operational costs. Every solution we build is designed to drive real business growth and create measurable impact.

At StriveGo, our mission goes beyond technology - it's about empowering Kenyan entrepreneurs to succeed. By combining AI innovation with our understanding of the Kenyan market, we help transform small businesses into competitive enterprises, create jobs, and position Kenya as a leader in AI-driven business solutions across Africa.

Questions Business Owners Ask About Growing Their Business

Get answers to the most common questions about how technology can help your business grow faster and reduce costs

How can I grow my business in Kenya with technology?

Technology can transform your business by automating customer service with WhatsApp bots (so you never miss a sale), creating a professional website that makes sales 24/7, building a mobile app that makes it easier for customers to buy from you, and integrating M-Pesa so customers can pay instantly. Our solutions help Kenyan businesses reduce operational costs by up to 40% while increasing sales through better customer service and online presence.

How much does it cost to automate my business with WhatsApp bots?

Our WhatsApp bot solutions start from KES 15,000-30,000 per month for small businesses, which includes automated customer responses, order processing, and basic M-Pesa integration. Professional plans with advanced features, analytics, and priority support are KES 45,000/month. Enterprise solutions with unlimited contacts and custom features start at KES 60,000/month. All plans help reduce customer service costs and increase sales.

Can you help my business accept M-Pesa payments online?

Yes! M-Pesa integration is one of our specialties. We integrate M-Pesa payments into your website, mobile app, or WhatsApp bot so customers can pay directly without leaving your platform. This makes it much easier for customers to buy from you and can increase your sales significantly.

How can I reduce my business costs with automation?

Our automation solutions can reduce your operational costs by handling customer inquiries 24/7 (no need to hire extra staff for night shifts), automating order processing and inventory management, reducing manual data entry errors, and freeing up your team to focus on growing the business. Many of our clients save 30-40% on operational costs while serving more customers.

Do you build websites that actually make sales, not just look good?

Absolutely! We build conversion-focused websites optimized for search engines (so customers find you on Google), fast-loading and mobile-responsive (so customers can buy on their phones), with M-Pesa payment integration, automated lead capture, and clear calls-to-action. Our websites are designed to make sales 24/7, not just showcase your business.

How long does it take to see results after implementing automation?

Simple WhatsApp bots and basic automation can be set up in 1-2 weeks, and you'll start seeing immediate benefits like 24/7 customer service and reduced response times. More complex solutions with custom integrations typically take 3-4 weeks. Most clients see increased sales and reduced costs within the first month of implementation. We'll provide a detailed timeline after understanding your specific business needs.

Can a small business compete with bigger companies using your solutions?

Yes! Our affordable AI and automation solutions help small businesses compete effectively. With automated 24/7 customer service, professional websites, mobile apps, and digital marketing, small businesses can offer the same level of service as larger companies at a fraction of the cost. Many of our SME clients are now competing successfully against much larger competitors.

What payment methods do you accept for your services?

We accept M-Pesa payments (most convenient for Kenyan businesses), bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. We can also invoice businesses for monthly or annual subscriptions, making it easy to budget for your business growth solutions.

Do you provide training so my team can use the systems?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training for all our solutions. This includes step-by-step documentation, video tutorials, and live training sessions to ensure you and your team can effectively use and manage the systems. We want you to succeed, so we make sure you're comfortable using everything we build for you.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer different levels of support depending on your plan. This includes email support, priority support, and 24/7 dedicated support for enterprise clients. We're committed to ensuring your systems run smoothly so your business can focus on growing, not fixing technical issues.
